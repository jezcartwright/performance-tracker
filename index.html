    Archive

    People removed from an org, kept on record. Each carries a permanent reference number (e.g. PT-…) so they stay identifiable even though their old work email belonged to the company they left. Reassign them to any company you manage and that number travels with them.

    Import org chart

    Drop in a spreadsheet (.xlsx or .csv) and the whole org is built — departments, people and reporting lines. Columns it understands: Name (or First / Last), Title, Email, Department, Manager (their manager's name or email), and an optional Leader flag. The person with no manager becomes the CEO.
